machine learning

I maintain mirrors of a couple popular machine learning datasets, including MNIST (both the standard and "fashion" versions) and CIFAR-10. I am also a contributor and author, respectively, of the Rust mnist and cifar-ten crates. Please feel free to use these mirrors, but try not to crush them with busy CI pipelines or something.
